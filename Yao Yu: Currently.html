The professor is in

The essential guide to turn your Ph.D into a job! The professor is in by Karen Kelsky is a book I am currently reading on how the academic job market works, what are the critical components making you a competitive job seeker, how to make informed career choices, protect financial security and mental health.

Some takeaways:
(the most important one) stop acting like a grad student!
The search committee is looking for a colleague peer. They want not a peon but a collegial, pleasant, and courteous peer. Your written and spoken self will give it away.
most important factors: publications, grants, targeted teaching experiences, impressive references.
