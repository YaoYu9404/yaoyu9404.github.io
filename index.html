Hi this is Yao! I am a Schmidt AI in science postdoc fellow at UCSD, working with David Sandwell, Sarah Gille and Julian McAuley. I received my PhD degree in Earth Sciences at the Scripps Institution of Oceanography in June 2023. Currently my research focus is to process and analyze SWOT satellite data and use machine learning to help understand small-scale ocean dynamics. My lastest work on SWOT gravity is now featured on the AVISO home page.

I am actively managing the Github repository SWOT_SIO and share codes/examples of SWOT data processing/analysis. I enjoy learning different things and I share my currect activities on the CURRENTLY page.
