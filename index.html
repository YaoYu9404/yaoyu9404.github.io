Hi this is Yao! I am currently a PhD student in geophysics at the Scripps Institution of Oceanography working with David Sandwell and Sarah Gille. I'm broadly interested in using satellite measurements to observe and understand the Earth, including but not limitted to: seafloor, ocean swells, tides and currents. I am particularly focusing on exploring seafloor's influence on oceanic signals.

Check the research page for more details on my research and field work for some cool pictures!
